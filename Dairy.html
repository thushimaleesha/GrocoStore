Dairy products are a staple in many diets and offer a wide range of nutritional benefits, including essential vitamins and minerals like calcium, vitamin D, and protein. In our shop we typically offers a variety of dairy products to cater to diverse consumer preferences and dietary needs. Here's a brief introduction to the main categories of dairy products available:

Milk

  • Whole Milk: Contains full-fat content, providing a rich taste and creamy texture.
  • Low-Fat and Skim Milk: Reduced-fat options for those seeking to lower their fat intake.
  • Flavored Milk: Includes chocolate, strawberry, and other flavored varieties.

Cheese & Butter

  • Hard Cheeses: Such as cheddar, Parmesan, and gouda, which are aged and have a firm texture.
  • Soft Cheeses: Including brie, camembert, and cream cheese, known for their creamy consistency.
  • Butter: Made from churned cream, available in salted and unsalted varieties.
  • Margarine: Plant-based alternative to butter, often used in baking and cooking

Cream

  • Heavy Cream: High-fat content, ideal for whipping and cooking.
  • Light Cream and Half-and-Half: Lower fat content, used in coffee and recipes.

Ice Cream & Frozen desserts

  • Traditional Ice Cream: Available in numerous flavors and styles.
  • Frozen Yogurt: Lower in fat than traditional ice cream, often with probiotic benefits.
  • Non-Dairy Ice Cream: Made from almond, coconut, soy
